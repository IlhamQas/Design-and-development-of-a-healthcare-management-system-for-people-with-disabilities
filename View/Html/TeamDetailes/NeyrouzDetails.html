Neyrouz Najem Al-Din

Speech and Hearing Specialist

With over 4 years of experience working with children of various ages and diagnoses, including speech and language disorders, fluency disorders, swallowing issues, ADHD, and autism spectrum disorder, she is dedicated to enhancing children’s communicative, linguistic, and cognitive development through personalized therapeutic approaches.

With expertise in assessment, diagnosis, and creating tailored treatment and family plans, she focuses on each child’s unique needs, strengths, and challenges. She holds a Bachelor’s degree in Speech and Hearing Therapy from Birzeit University and has completed various specialized certifications in child apraxia, DIRFloortime, autism training, and more.
